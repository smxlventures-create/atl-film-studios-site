About

A working production studio, in Atlanta.

Operated by Swirl Films — an active film and television production company that builds these sets for its own shows.

ATL Film Studios is the production facility behind Swirl Films' originals. We built six pre-dressed sets, an LED volume wall, and a cyc to support our own productions. When our calendar allows, we open the doors to outside crews.

That's the difference between us and the dominant Atlanta multi-set listings: we're a production company first. The stages are built to the standards a working production needs because we use them ourselves. The studio manager speaks production because that's his day job, not a customer-service role.

Swirl Films

Swirl Films is a full-service production company with originals distributed on TUBI, BET+, and other major streaming platforms. The company has been in business for over a decade producing feature-length films, episodic content, music videos, and branded content. ATL Film Studios is the physical home of those productions.

Why we opened the doors

Production-grade sets sit idle between our own shoots. Other Atlanta crews need them. We charge industry rates, post pricing publicly, and don't bury sync-sound capacity behind contact-for-quote theater. If you want to shoot a courtroom scene, a hospital scene, and an interrogation in the same day — all sync-sound, one address, one flat rate — we built the studio for that.

The studio

  • Six pre-dressed permanent sets (courtroom, hospital room, hospital corridor, police bullpen, interrogation, plus dressing variants)
  • One LED volume wall, modular, Unreal Engine ready
  • One hard cyc wall, clean white sweep
  • Production lighting grid throughout
  • Sync-sound rated stages
  • Drive-in loading bay
  • Hair / makeup / wardrobe, holding, kitchenette, base camp space
  • Free crew parking
  • 24/7 access available
