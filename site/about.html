ABOUT THE STUDIO

Built by Producers.
For Producers.

ATL Film Studios was created to solve a persistent production headache: the cost and time of custom set construction for common episodic scenes.

The Swirl Films Heritage

As an active, working production company, Swirl Films understands the pressures of modern episodic and commercial shoots. Under the leadership of founder Eric Tomosunas, Swirl has produced hundreds of hours of content for networks, streamers, and independent brands.

ATL Film Studios is our physical manifestation of production efficiency. By housing eight distinct, pre-dressed standing sets under one roof, we enable productions to execute multiple scene transitions in a single day, eliminating the downtime of company moves and rebuilding sets.

Our Mission & Commitment

We are dedicated to providing a professional, distraction-free environment for film, television, and commercial crews. Our facility is strictly reserved for professional production work — ensuring your sound records are clean and your team can work in privacy.

We prioritize diversity, equity, and inclusion in our hiring and vendor selections, actively working to support the growth of the local Georgia film ecosystem.

FOUNDER PROFILE

Eric Tomosunas

Eric Tomosunas is an established film and television producer with over two decades of experience in the entertainment industry. As the founder of Swirl Films, he has established the company as one of the most prolific independent production entities in the Southeast.

His vision for ATL Film Studios was born from practical necessity: creating a centralized, high-quality, and cost-effective hub for shooting procedural, medical, and virtual production scenes in Atlanta.

A working production studio, not a venue. Built for shows, available by the day. Operated by Swirl Films.
