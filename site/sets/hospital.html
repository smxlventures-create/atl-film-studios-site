Hospital Patient Room

A fully dressed medical set featuring a patient room and adjacent waiting corridor. Complete with authentic hospital bed, vitals monitor, and medical props.

Book on Peerspace Request Quote

Medical drama, solved.

Our hospital set includes a fully furnished patient room and a matching hospital corridor. Designed to look like a modern hospital, it's perfect for medical dramas, commercials, and industrial videos.

The patient room features an electric hospital bed with working controls, an IV stand with drip bags, a rolling overbed table, and a patient vitals monitor (mock screen). The walls are painted in institutional hospital green and feature authentic medical gas outlets and call buttons.

The adjacent corridor is styled with commercial-grade hospital doors, institutional waiting chairs, handrails, and exit signs. This layout allows you to shoot a continuous take from the hallway directly into the patient's room, creating a seamless and convincing sense of space.
