STANDING SET 07 / PRISON CELL

Prison Cell Set

State prison cell standing set with double metal bunks, a mock stainless steel toilet/sink combo, and heavy concrete-textured walls.

Book on Peerspace Request Quote

Dressed for drama.

A state prison cell standing set designed for long-term confinement scenes. Features double metal bunks, a mock stainless steel prison toilet and sink combo unit, concrete-textured walls, and a heavy steel door with a sliding food slot. Perfect for prison dramas, correctional institution scenes, and music videos.

The set is built inside our sound-insulated stage, offering full control over lighting and acoustics. The heavy steel door and concrete walls allow you to capture highly realistic sound effects and high-contrast, dramatic shadows.

Photos are being shot this month. For a walkthrough video or to schedule an in-person scout of the stage, request a quote and our studio manager will send you the latest video clip from set.
