About this space.

A working-precinct build for episodic and feature crime drama. Two connected spaces, shot in continuity.

Detective bullpen

Multi-desk squad room — four detective desks with period-appropriate monitors, filing cabinets, precinct seal on the wall, active case board with photos, papers, and string tacks (a real cold-case wall, not a token sheet of paper), kitchenette with fridge and coffee in back, two-tone gray walls with blue accent. Industrial ceiling with exposed conduit reads as institutional / government building.

Interrogation room

The iconic shot. Single metal table, two metal chairs, single overhead bulb, two-way mirror on one wall, heavy door on the opposite wall. Purple-lavender lower walls + tan upper walls — distinctive palette that reads moody and slightly off-kilter on camera.

Pricing wedge: other Atlanta multi-set listings charge 2-hr minimum PER set, +1 hr per additional set used. Ours is a flat hourly rate that includes both rooms — productions save hours of clock-watching surcharge.
