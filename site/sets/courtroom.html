STANDING SET 01 · COURTROOM

Pre-dressed Courtroom

A complete, camera-ready courtroom set inside our insulated stage. Elevated judge's bench, witness stand, counsel tables, and matching gallery pews.

Book on Peerspace Request Quote

Dressed for drama.

Our courtroom set is built and styled to match modern county and federal courtrooms. No green-screen workarounds or expensive prop rentals — the space is fully dressed and ready to shoot.

The set features a heavy wood judge's bench with adjacent witness stand and court reporter desk. Two counselor tables with matching chairs sit before the bar. The gallery contains three rows of authentic wooden pews, capable of holding up to 30 extras.

Every wall is finished with rich wood paneling and architectural molding. Details include American and Georgia state flags, a state seal emblem, and a working swinging bar gate. The layout allows for clean master shots, cross-coverage on counsel, and dramatic low-angle close-ups on the bench.
