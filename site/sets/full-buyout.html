About this space.

Swirl Films Atlanta is a working production studio operated by an active film and TV production company. We built and dress these sets for our own shows; when our calendar allows, we open the doors.

The buyout gives you the entire facility under one flat rate: every dressed set, the LED volume wall, the cyc, and the supporting amenities. Productions that would normally make three company moves (precinct → courtroom → hospital) shoot all three at one address, on one call sheet, with no per-set time surcharges.

What's included on a buyout day

  • Courtroom — full judge's bench, witness stand, gallery seating, US + state flag dress, working banker's lamp, acoustic-panel walls
  • Hospital suite — patient room (bed, vitals monitor, IV pole, sink station) plus connected corridor (waiting bench, doors to additional rooms)
  • Police precinct — multi-desk bullpen with active case board, precinct seal, period-appropriate detective stations
  • Interrogation room — two-way mirror, single overhead bulb, heavy door
  • LED volume wall — modular wall stage for virtual production, driving plates, hero environments
  • Cyc wall — clean white sweep for product, lookbook, or stylized inserts
  • Drive-in loading bay — full-height rolling door, ground-level load-in
