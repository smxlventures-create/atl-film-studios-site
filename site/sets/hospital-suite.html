About this space.

Two connected pieces — a patient room and a corridor that opens through to it. Pull a patient out of the room into the hall in one continuous take, or use each space standalone.

Pricing context: the premium comparable Atlanta hospital set charges $550-$750/hr. Ours is $275/hr — half the rate — because we're an active production company running this as a working studio, not a prop-house revenue line.

Patient room

Adjustable hospital bed, vitals monitor on rolling cart, IV pole, anatomical skeleton model (the spine model is a cinematic detail), bedside lamp, visitor chair, sink/wash station on the opposite wall. Two-tone walls (clinical green lower / cream upper with horizontal divider) read instantly as hospital. Coverage runs wide-to-tight: master of the bed, OTS from the visitor chair, tight on the patient, reverse on the monitor.

Corridor

Waiting-area bench seating, side table, plant, door leading to the patient room (visible white sweep through the door — useful for "into the OR" coverage), industrial ceiling with exposed pipe, safety posters and photo frames, fluorescent overhead lighting.
