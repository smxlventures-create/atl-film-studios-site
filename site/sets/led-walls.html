About this space.

A modular LED volume wall — multiple panels reconfigurable per shot. We've used it for in-camera VFX on our own narrative productions, for stylized music video environments, and for car driving plates.

Useful for

  • Car driving plates / picture car — without location permits, low-loaders, or insurance gymnastics
  • Replacing greenscreen on narrative / episodic work
  • In-camera VFX commercials — product backgrounds, hero environments
  • Music videos — hero looks without travel
  • Brand campaigns where every setup needs a different visual context

The tiered rate

Mirrors the dominant Atlanta LED facility model. Small crews on test or single-camera days get a value tier; full productions with grip and electric load-in get the production tier. No quote-on-request theater.

  • Value tier — $150/hr (1–5 crew) — for small-crew tests, content R&D, single-camera shoots
  • Production tier — $150/hr (6–15 crew) — for full productions with grip and electric load-in
