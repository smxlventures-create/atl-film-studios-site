FAQ

The production questions, answered.

If something's not here, call Tyrell at 470-231-8971 or email us.

Are the stages sync-sound rated?
Yes. Every stage here is built and treated for sync-sound. Many comparable Atlanta multi-set listings quote MOS at the headline rate and charge extra for soundproofing. Our published rates are the sync-sound rates.
What's the COI requirement and minimum coverage?
Productions over 8 crew need a Certificate of Insurance naming Swirl Films Atlanta as additional insured. Minimum $1M general liability is standard. We can send a sample COI on request when you book.
Can we bring our own grip and electric?
Yes. Bring your own kits, or rent ours. We have a production lighting kit (Aputure 600d + 300d + accessories) at $500/day and a grip & electric package at $250/day. Custom requirements: let us know in your quote request.
Do you provide a studio manager on-site?
Yes — Tyrell is on-site for the duration of your booking, included in every rate. He handles room access, sets the grid for your call, coordinates any add-ons, and is your point of contact during the shoot.
Is there a base camp / production office area?
Yes. We have a dedicated production office space with desks, WiFi, and seating for your line producer, script supervisor, and continuity team. The kitchenette is shared.
What's the cancellation policy?
Most sets: Moderate — 100% refund 7+ days out · 50% refund 3–7 days · no refund inside 72 hours.

LED Walls: Strict — 100% refund 14+ days out · 50% refund 7–14 days · no refund inside 7 days. LED bookings block specialized resources.
Can we hold a date before booking?
Yes, with a 50% non-refundable deposit. The hold guarantees the date is yours and removes it from public availability until you confirm.
Do you offer multi-day discounts?
Yes. Full Buyout: −10% for 2 days, −15% for 3+ days. Other sets: discounts available on request for multi-day or week-long bookings — send dates in the quote request.
Is there parking for crew vehicles? Trucks?
Free on-site crew parking. Production trucks and trailers: yes, but confirm vehicle size with Tyrell during booking — the drive-in bay accommodates most production vehicles but not over-height trailers.
What's the load-in bay door dimension?
Full-height rolling door for drive-in load-in, ground-level. Specific dimensions on request — Tyrell can confirm during a walk-through.
Are minors and animals allowed on set?
Minors: yes, with a guardian and applicable GA child labor permits. Animals: yes, with advance notice and a $200 surcharge for additional cleaning. Service animals at no charge.
Pyro, open flame, stunts policy?
Pyro, open flame, and stunts require 7-day advance approval, a licensed coordinator, and additional COI coverage. Standard fog and atmosphere is fine without special approval.
Can we modify or paint the existing sets?
No. The sets stay dressed for repeat bookings. Small repositioning of moveable furniture is OK with studio manager approval. Custom dressing (e.g. swapping case-board photos in the precinct) is available on advance request — $250 flat per custom dressing change.
Sales tax on rentals in Georgia?
Real-property rental (studio space) is generally not subject to GA sales tax. Tangible add-ons (equipment rental, props) may be taxable. Tax handling will be itemized on your final quote.
Do you have lighting and grip packages?
Yes — standard production lighting kit (Aputure 600d + 300d + accessories) at $500/day, grip & electric basics (C-stands, flags, sandbags, stingers) at $250/day. Add to your quote request or to your booking after confirmation.
What's included in every booking?
Full set use during your booked time · studio manager on-site · basic house lighting (overhead grid + fixtures only — bring or rent kit for cinema lighting) · WiFi 1 Gbps · trash and restroom supplies · standard cleaning post-shoot.
Can I tour the studio first?
Absolutely. Schedule a walk-through with Tyrell — call 470-231-8971 or submit a quote request and note "scout only." Walk-throughs are free.
Are you on Peerspace and Giggster?
Yes — we list on both for platform-mediated bookings. Direct booking through this site saves the platform fee (19–20%) and lets us be more flexible on rates, add-ons, and scheduling.
