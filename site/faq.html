Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about booking, scheduling, and working on stage at ATL Film Studios.

What are your rates and minimum booking times?

Our base rate is $150 per hour with a strict 3-hour minimum booking. For bookings of 8 hours or more, we apply a 10% multi-hour discount. All rates are inclusive of all eight standing sets and both LED volume walls.

What is your cancellation policy?

We adhere to a professional, industry-standard cancellation policy. Bookings canceled more than 7 days prior to the scheduled start time receive a full 100% refund. Cancellations made between 7 days and 24 hours prior receive a 50% refund. Bookings canceled less than 24 hours before the shoot are non-refundable.

Are the stages quiet enough for sync sound?

Yes. Our facility is a dedicated production stage, fully insulated and isolated from street noise. It is designed and rated specifically for capturing clean sync-sound dialogue.

Are pets allowed on set?

Animals are allowed on stage only if they are trained, working animal actors required for the specific scene being filmed, and must be accompanied by a certified handler. General pets or companion animals are strictly prohibited on the facility grounds for safety and liability reasons.

Is there secure parking and loading facilities?

Yes. We feature a fully gated, secure parking lot that accommodates over 20 crew cars and multiple heavy production trucks. For gear loading, we have a 14-foot grade-level roll-up door that opens directly onto the stage floor.
